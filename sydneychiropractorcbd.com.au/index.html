Back Pain Treatment in the CBD

Back pain is a common malady the world over. In Australia, it is estimated that around 4 million or 1 in every 6 people in the country had or still experiences back pain. This is why back pain treatment remains one of the most in-demand therapies at Clinic 27, and one where we have extensive expertise.

There are various factors that can cause back pain, including a sedentary lifestyle, accidents or injuries, strained muscles, stress and other lifestyle-related reasons.

Whatever the cause of your back pain, our team at Clinic 27 will apply the latest chiropractic treatment methods to ensure you find the relief you need.

Carpal Tunnel Syndrome Treatment & Diagnosis in the CBD

The age of technology has brought many conveniences with it, including the Internet and digital devices. However, the convenience of technology comes at a great price as spending hours clicking away on your computer or holding a mobile phone, tapping and swiping can all wreak havoc on yourhands and arms.

One of the most common ill effects of our digital activities is carpal tunnel syndrome (CTS).This condition manifests itself in the form of a numbing and tingling sensation in your hand and arm. With CTS, you also feel like youre losing your ability to grip objects like pencils or coffee cup handles. And while surgery is a treatment option, it also comes with risks.

We offer a safer, gentle, non-invasive method of treating CTS effectively at Clinic 27. We treat CTS right at the source so you can experience immediate and long-lasting pain relief.

Neck Pain Treatment in the CBD

Similar to back pain, neck pain is a very common problem among Australians and people the world over.

There are several causes of neck pain, although it usually arises from muscle strain due to poor posture, especially in relation to the use of electronic devices like mobile phones and computers.

Other common causes of neck pain include poor sleeping posture, neck trauma or injury, performing tasks that force you to crane your neck, stress and anxiety. It can also be caused by cervical spondylosis (neck arthritis), a slipped disc and whiplash. Neck pain can resolve itself or linger for long, depending on the cause.

Whatever may be the root cause of your neck pain, our team of chiropractors at Clinic 27 can provide effective neck pain treatment after a careful and thorough assessment of your condition.

Vertigo Treatment in the CBD

Have you experienced dizzy spells characterised by a sense of movement or spinning sensation even when youre not moving? If you have, especially if it occurred on more than one occasion, you may be suffering from vertigo. This condition accounts for around54 percentof cases of dizziness.

Vertigo is not dangerous on its own, but it can disrupt everyday life and prevent you from performing simple tasks. It is also sometimes accompanied by nausea and vomiting things you wouldnt want to experience any day. The thing with vertigo is that there are several factors that could cause it, so treating this condition is somewhat tricky.

At Clinic 27, you will undergo a thorough assessment and consultation before we proceed with any type ofvertigo treatment.

There are different factors that could cause headaches and migraine, including stress,genetics, hormonal imbalance and so on.

At Clinic 27, our team of chiropractic professionals can provide gentle and non-invasiveheadache and migraine treatmentto manage and relieve pain.

Sciatica Treatment in the CBD

Sciatica is a health condition known to cause pain in the back and legs. The pain sciatica causes can become unbearable and debilitating when the pain moves along the sciatic nerve path.

Obesity, a sedentary lifestyle and ageing are some of the most common causes of sciatica pain, as these can all put extra pressure on the spine. Meanwhile, diabetes can also increase the risk of sciatic nerve damage, usually manifesting as progressive numbness, weakness or tingling in the feet and lower legs.

However, there are cases where sciatica can improve on its own over time. But if what you want is immediate relief from sciatica pain, we offer a safe and effective non-surgicaltreatment option for sciaticahere at Clinic 27.

Scoliosis Treatment in the CBD

The condition known as scoliosis involves the abnormal sideways curvature of the spine. Depending on the severity of the condition, scoliosis usually requires surgery to correct.

However, even milder cases of scoliosis can be extremely painful as it affects the bodys anatomy. The more pronounced the spinal curvature, the more difficult it would be to breathe as the condition can affect lung capacity.

If you suffer from this condition, you can benefit fromchiropractic scoliosis treatmentby our team of chiropractic health experts at Clinic 27.
